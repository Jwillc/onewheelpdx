Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How difficult is it to learn?
Most people get comfortable with basic riding within 30-60 minutes. We provide safety gear if you need it to make learning easier. The Pint X is actually the easiest model to learn on thanks to its smaller size and simpler controls.
Where can I ride in Portland?
Portland has amazing Onewheel terrain! Including nice flat roads, parks, and an abundance of bike lanes.
What's your cancellation policy?
All rentals are handles through friendwitha.com, see their policies before confiriming the rental.
What if I damage the Onewheel?
Minor scratches from normal use are expected. The board can handle a beating, so don't worry. For significant damage FriendWithA provides insurance. We recommend practicing in open areas until you're comfortable.
How does pickup/dropoff work?
We're located in NE Portland off of Alberta Street. You'll pick up and return the Onewheel at my apartment. Convient no-contact pickup, just like AirBNB but for Onewheel.
